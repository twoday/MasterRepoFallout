  • The Beginnings of CoFrisson!

    CoFrisson was started in Feb 2013 by two friends who wanted to share their love and enthusiasm with others. The current development is based between Regina,Saskatchewan (Canada) and North Manchester, Indiana (USA). The project was started by Kebb Marcum and Markus Newhart while they have been coding Fallout Chronicle, their first partnered game. With plans on creating new and creative projects in the future, they also are wanting to expand their knowledge in the gaming field.
